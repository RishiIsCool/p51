  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons

  1. Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica

  2. Cicadas can't eat, getting nutrients from tree roots

  3. Some cicadas could explode with fungus

  2. Their eyes are different to normal bears

Pigeon

  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities

  3. Pigeons are highly sociable animals

Kodiak Bear

  1. Kodiak bears are the largest bears in the world

  2. There are about 3500 Kodiak bears.

  3. They have been isolated on the Kodiak Archipelago since at least the last ice age
